What are we upto.


We are creating a community of passionate and motivated product management students who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to succeed in their careers. We strive to create meaningful opportunities for students to develop their product management skills through workshops, speaker events, case competitions, and networking events. Our goal is to empower students to become innovative and effective product managers who can lead their teams to success in the ever-evolving tech industry.
