    • Why a Podcast?


    As a student organization dedicated to fostering knowledge and growth in the field of Product Management, we are launching our very own podcast on this fascinating subject. Our podcast will serve as a platform to bring you insightful discussions, expert interviews, and valuable perspectives on all things related to Product Management.

    Well, we believe that podcasts are an incredibly powerful medium for sharing knowledge and facilitating meaningful conversations. They offer a convenient and accessible way to consume content, whether you're commuting, exercising, or simply taking a moment to relax. With our podcast, we aim to provide you with valuable insights and inspiration that you can enjoy anytime, anywhere.
