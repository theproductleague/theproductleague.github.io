Our Vision

Our vision is to create a community of passionate and motivated product management students who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to succeed in their careers.

We aim to foster a collaborative and inclusive environment where students can learn, network, and grow as product managers through various engagement activities like case competitions, networking fairs, book-reading sessions etc.

Why become a member

With our membership program, you can gain access to special advantages and join a dynamic community of like-minded people. Our membership offers everything you need to advance in your career as product manager, from networking opportunities to premium tools.

Career Networking

To provide students with opportunities to connect with top employers, showcase their skills and experience & land their dream jobs.

Productathons

Productathons enable the member to foster creativity and problem-solving through real-world challenges and expert feedback

1:1 Mentoring

Personalized guidance & support for members from experienced product managers to help you achieve your career goals.

Book-Reading

Each month, our exclusive group of members will dive deep into the most compelling product books out there, and engage in lively discussions.

Panel Discussions

Attend speaker events and trainings on topics related to product management, such as product design, market research etc.

Newsletters

Join our community of subscribers and gain exclusive access to expert content that will elevate your knowledge and skills.
